Quiet confession from 8 years in retail VM:

The best store I ever launched wasn't the one with the biggest budget.

It was the one where:
→ Vendors were briefed 45 days in advance
→ Fixtures arrived 5 days before launch (not 5 hours)
→ We did a mock setup before the real one
→ Every display had a clear commercial objective

Retail VM success isn't about aesthetics alone. It's about execution.

Your displays are only as good as your vendor partnerships.

Build the vendor relationships first. The stunning stores follow.
