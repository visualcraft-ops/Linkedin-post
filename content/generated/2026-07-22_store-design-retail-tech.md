Here's a store design & retail tech reminder that costs ₹0:

1. Visit your vendor's workshop once a quarter (trust builds in person)
2. Share visual briefs, not just specs (vendors aren't mind-readers)
3. Pay on time — it's the best negotiation tool you'll ever have
4. Document what works (future you will thank present you)

Simple? Yes.
Does everyone do it? No.

That's the edge in vendor management.
